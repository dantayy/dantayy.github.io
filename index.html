Playing hard by working hard...

Hi there! I'm Nick, an aspiring game designer who wants to specialize in level design. Gaming's been a part of my life for as long as I can remember, and I've always wanted to give back to the industry that's given so much to me. After earning my degree in Game Design & Development at the Rochester Institute of Technology in the Spring of 2020 I landed a job at Lockheed Martin, and I'm currently there as a Software Engineer 2.

At the moment I'm looking for a full-time position in game design and/or development. Below is a shortlist of projects that pertain to that aspiration, as well as some contact info. No matter how or why you ended up here, I hope you'll find something that piques your interest!

(HINT: click the project images to take you to the relevant project site)

Project Overlord

With the release of Unreal Engine 5 I wanted to take the opportunity to learn and develop a standalone gaming project. So, taking inspiration from Call of Duty and Radio Commander, I set out to code, design and implement a short, dialogue-driven AC-130 rail shooter. C++ code is kept up to date on my github, and hopefully I'll have a playable demo available for everyone to try soon!

Sleep Patrol Alpha Level Design

Early in 2021 I was asked by a friend to assist with creating some new content for their group's mobile platforming game. I collaborated with the team to figure out what would work best for a second level in their game and applied my level design know-how and feedback from them to iterate on layouts to produce a final product that is live on app stores now!

Halo Forge Projects

Learning how to use Halo's forge mode as a kid was one of the pivotal moments in my life that led me to pursue game design as a career, and with Halo Infinite's iteration fresh in my mind, I've had a burst of inspiration that I wanted to run with and record to help catalogue my processes and further showcase my passion for level design in a space that comes to me naturally. Current and future projects will be showcased in the linked playlist, so check out what's there and stay tuned for what's to come!
